About Me

About Me

I am Abdulla Al Farabi from Bangladesh. I was born and grew up in a small town called Narsingdi, situated just beside the capital of Bangladesh, Dhaka. I received my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from the Bangladesh Army University of Engineering and Technology.

Right after my graduation, I joined as a trainee at Alema Textile Limited (MIM Group) on 20 August 2021. Three months later, I got a promotion to assistant engineer (electrical) in dyeing production and still now I am continuing my job in this company.

Along with my professional career, I am a very enthusiastic, hard-working, and dedicated person.

I believe that Perseverance, Hardworking, and dedication can bring everyone utmost success

Career

Assistant Engineer (Electrical), Alema Textile Limited (MIM Group).

I started my professional career by joining as a trainee at Alema Textile Limited (MIM Group). Three months later, I got a promotion to assistant engineer (electrical) in dyeing production. Very beginning of my duty, I was responsible for maintaining sub-station equipment and giving reports to the higher authority if any fault occurred. I am now responsible for maintaining and operating various sub-station equipment like Diesel Generators, transformers, AVR, Relay, HT and LT Panel, etc in the dyeing. Besides, I did a 30 days internship at Katakhali 50MW peaking power plant where I worked and associated with power generation, national grid system, compressed air system, cooling water system, oil water treatment, diesel engine arrangement, condensate treatment system, basic IC engine, electrical system, control & protection system and fire fighting system

Industrial Intern, Katakhali Power Station

Supervised electrical works when plant was in installing phase. Provide prompt responses to inquiries whenever there is a breakdown and resolve the issue as quickly as possible. I was the team lead of Innovation and Development team. I worked with a cross - functional leadership team to drive the operational process which included setting goals and measurements to monitor performance and financial plans.
